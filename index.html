About Me

Learn a bit more about who I am and what drives me

Hello! I'm a passionate software developer with a strong focus on building reliable, scalable, and efficient full stack applications. From backend systems to frontend interfaces, I love creating complete solutions that solve real-world problems and offer seamless user experiences.

Before entering the world of software, I served as a firefighter and EMT — a role that taught me the value of teamwork, clear communication, and staying calm under pressure. Working in high-stakes environments helped me develop critical thinking, adaptability, and the ability to remain focused no matter the challenge. These qualities now shape how I approach software development and problem-solving every day.

While working full-time as a first responder, I pursued and earned my Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering. Balancing long shifts and emergency calls with rigorous coursework wasn't easy — but it reflects my commitment to growth, discipline, and my passion for building meaningful technology.

When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking or brushing up on my photography skills. These activities help me recharge and stay creative, which I bring back to my development work.

I believe that great software is built on clean code, thoughtful architecture, and user-centered design. My goal is to combine technical excellence with real-world perspective to create solutions that are not only functional, but impactful.
